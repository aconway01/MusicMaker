Welcome to Music Maker, where there's everything you need to make your own music! Start by selecting the Song Key tab. You'll find the chords you should use with your song key along with the notes in the scale to play and sing along with it. Don't know your chords? Try the Chords tab where you'll find the notes that all your chords consist of. Finally, use the Metronome tab to play your song and stay in time to the beat of the music!
